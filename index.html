How it works

Three steps.
Done.

Designed to be fast, frictionless and elegant — for both tenants and agents.

01
Set your preferences
Choose your listing category, city, budget and must-have features. Takes under a minute. The deck adapts immediately.
02
Swipe through listings
Swipe right on properties you love, left on those you don't. Every listing is from a verified agent with a badge and rating displayed on the card.
03
Message instantly
Nest a property and open a direct chat with your agent — no forms, no waiting. Book a viewing in minutes. Rate your experience after.
